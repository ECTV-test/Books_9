[[CHAPTER: Chapter 1]]

TWO THANKSGIVING DAY GENTLEMEN

There is one day for us. 

On this day, Americans go home. 

We eat and remember old times. 

We love this day. 

President Roosevelt gives it to us. 

We hear about the Puritans. 

We don't remember them well. 

We think we can beat them. 

Plymouth Rocks sounds familiar. 

Many eat chicken now. 

Turkey is too expensive. 

The big city loves Thanksgiving Day. 

It is the last Thursday in November. 

It is a special day for Americans.

Here is a story about a tradition. 

Stuffy Pete sits on a bench. 

He sits there every Thanksgiving Day. 

He sits at 1 o'clock. 

Things happen to him there. 

Today, Stuffy Pete is not hungry. 

He just ate a big meal. 

His face is round and full. 

His coat is tight. 

Buttons pop off his coat. 

He feels cold air on his skin. 

He ate oysters, turkey, and pudding. 

He ate chicken salad and pie. 

He ate ice cream too. 

Stuffy Pete is very full. 

He sits and feels happy.

The meal was a surprise. 

He walked past a big house. 

Two old ladies live there. 

They love traditions. 

They give food to hungry people. 

Stuffy Pete got a big meal. 

He walked to the park after.

Stuffy Pete looks around. 

He sees the Old Gentleman. 

The Old Gentleman comes to him. 

Every Thanksgiving, he finds Stuffy Pete. 

He takes Stuffy to eat dinner. 

The Old Gentleman likes traditions. 

He wants to start one. 

He is thin and tall. 

He wears black clothes. 

He uses a big cane.

Stuffy Pete wants to run. 

He cannot move. 

The Old Gentleman speaks. 

"Good morning," he says. 

"I am happy to see you. 

Come with me for dinner."

Stuffy Pete feels sad. 

The Old Gentleman is kind. 

He has no family. 

He lives alone. 

He grows flowers in winter. 

He walks in spring. 

He stays in New Jersey in summer. 

He feeds Stuffy in autumn.

Stuffy Pete looks at him. 

The Old Gentleman is happy. 

Stuffy makes a noise. 

He says, "Thank you, sir. 

I am very hungry."

Stuffy Pete is full. 

But he goes with the Old Gentleman. 

They go to a restaurant. 

The waiters know them. 

The Old Gentleman is happy. 

Stuffy Pete eats a lot. 

He eats turkey and pies. 

He eats until he is very full.

Stuffy Pete thanks the Old Gentleman. 

He leaves the restaurant. 

He walks around a corner. 

He falls to the ground.

An ambulance comes for Stuffy Pete. 

He goes to the hospital. 

They check him for sickness.

Later, another ambulance comes. 

The Old Gentleman is in it. 

He goes to the hospital too.

A doctor talks to a nurse. 

"That old gentleman is hungry," he says. 

"He has not eaten for three days."
